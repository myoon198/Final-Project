About

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Mira and I am an interactive designer and artist based in Washington DC. I studied at the University of Maryland, College Park from 2013 - 2018 and am aspiring to go graduate school in 2020. While in undergrad, I majored in studio art and specialized in digital media. While I love to make art with more traditional mediums such as paint, I have grown interested in using digital mediums. My work revolves around making digital experiences like games or websites as well as illustrations, paintings, and intermedia works.

When I'm not working, I'm probably baking, making chocolate, sleeping, or petting my dog. If this post-undergrad job search doesn't work out, I'm going to get a job as a dog-sitter.
